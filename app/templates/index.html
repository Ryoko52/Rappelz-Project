TIER CLASS NOTES
S Mercenary Exceptional damage output and survivability combination.
S Deadeye Top-tier ranged DPS with critical strike synergy.
S Slayer Highest burst damage; dominates PvE and PvP.
S Void Mage Incredible AoE damage; essential for group content.
A Master Breeder Pet synergy specialists; strong in coordinated teams.
A Overlord Superior survivability and crowd control abilities.
B Magus Strong elemental magic; good utility for groups.
B Cardinal Balanced healer with support capabilities.
B Templar Solid defensive tank with healing support.
C Berserker High risk/reward class; requires careful play.
C Beast Master Pet-focused playstyle; situational effectiveness.
C War Kahuna Niche support role; limited endgame viability.

D Marksman Outclassed by Deadeye; poor scaling late-game.
