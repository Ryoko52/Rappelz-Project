Gameplay

The gameplay is very intuitive: cast a trigger spell to proc Vortex Magic on mobs, then follow up with another AoE for amplified damage, boosted further with Magical Requisition .

Example rotation: Pull mobs with Mighty Energy Beat → cast Magical Requisition → stack mobs → cast Arctic Blast to proc Vortex Magic → follow up with Rainbow Field for amplified AoE damage.

Gear

Progression Path

  1. R2 helmet +20 (all skills +1)
  2. R6 +15 gear from hidden sanctuary
  3. Circus weapons and accessories from Circus dungeon
  4. Parallel World gear (either trash stats 2 slots with your main skill, or max slots in each gear but trash skill, depends on what you could find)
  5. Island of gods weapons and rings
  6. DD Accessories and Yushiva belt
  7. Lava gear (boots, gloves, armor, helmet)
  8. Medal
  9. Emblem
