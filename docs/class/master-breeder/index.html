Master Breeder

Summoner Pet Class

Overview

RoleDPS / Tank / Heal (thanks to pets)
DifficultyEasy
StrengthsVery strong early game thanks to amazing synergy with Ethereal Pixie and Crystal Golem
WeaknessesOnce you reach 220 contents, the performance drops significantly
Why Play ItIf you want to experience the summoner theme of the game, this is the class!

Rotation

Potency makes Master Breeder excel with 2 types of pets:

  • Pets that scale with Max Mana
  • Pets that scale with Max HP

Which is why the most famous combo for Master Breeder is Ethereal Pixie + Crystal Golem.

  • Ethereal Pixie is your main DPS
  • Crystal Golem is your tank

Main Skills of Pets

Best Skills to Use on Ethereal Pixie

  • Elemental Harmony Staged toggle skill
  • Blazing Storm AoE spell with DoT
  • Steam Bomb AoE spell
  • Scalding Rain AoE spell
  • Phoenix Impact Triggers Kindling passive that boosts INT/M.Atk
  • Shadow Illusion Dethreat skill
  • Encouragement +200% INT
  • Healing spells: Purifying Flames, Phoenix Embrace, Undine Blessing

Important passives: Otherworldly Wisdom, Mysticism (Staged), Kindling

Best Skills to Use on Crystal Golem

  • Ruby Eruption High threat AoE
  • Topaz Tantrum AoE stun
  • Diamond Dust AoE
  • Titanite Toggle that increases Threat, HP and Vitality
  • Wonderstone Damage taken removed from Mana
  • Threatening Essence Threat toggle
  • Encouragement +100% STR/VIT

Important passives: Rhodochrosite Might, Diamond Skin, Razor Sharp Iolite, Peridot Pain (Staged)

Gear

Progression Path

  1. R2 helmet +20 (all skills +1)
  2. 1 Crossbow with 6% P.Atk, 120 STR + Magewall with 6% INT
  3. +5 Creature Mastery ring and Bear belt from Circus
  4. Parallel World gear (either max slots with trash skill, or Potency gear with trash stats / low slots whatever you can find in the Auction House)
  5. Island of Gods 2H Staff for your Ethereal Pixie (1300+ M.Atk, 38+ CP), 2H Mace 6% P.Atk, max HP for Crystal Golem
  6. Crit Power Deco set from Altar or Lava Gear
  7. Yushiva set for increased stats and more Crit Power
  8. Medal with Potency
  9. Emblem with Potency

Optimization

The list above is a high-level overview. For maximum efficiency, follow this upgrade order:

  1. S5 Ethereal Pixie
  2. S5 Crystal Golem
  3. Boots +24 as soon as possible (+25 if you can afford it)
  4. Helmet +24
  5. +24 weapon on Ethereal Pixie
  6. Necklace with % increased damage vs type
  7. Sand Cloak +20
  8. Yushiva Belt on both pets if Stage 5

Tips

  1. Get a Defense Mastery set to increase the damage reduction on your buff.
  2. Awaken all your accessories and Crossbow/Magewall with CP.
  3. If you want to farm Rupees in Lake Kaia, it is better to have 2 Wind Pixies S0.
  4. Move your pets with Shift + Left Click.
  5. Purse of Invocation from the Cash Shop is a huge boost to your pets (500 coins).
  6. If your pets are Stage 5, you can put a Yushiva Belt on them to increase all skills by 1 level.
