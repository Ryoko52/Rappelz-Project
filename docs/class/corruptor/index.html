Corruptor

Tier S

Overview

RoleDPS / subtank
DifficultyEasy
StrengthsHas burst damage and amazing sustain
WeaknessesOnly 3 main damage skills with ~30sec CD (can go lower with helmet+24 and + cards)
Why Play ItIf you like cool sword animations, easy to progress melee character, this is the class!

Skills

Main Skills

  • Phantasm
  • Hellstorm
  • Deadly Dancing
  • Apocalypse
  • Phase Blades (this is the skill you aim to have in all your gear)

Useful Skills

  • Gloom Aura for pulling
  • Binding Dust and Smoke Bomb for more dodging and tanking capabilities
  • Sudden Rush for maximum speeeeeeeeeeed!

Defensive Buffs

  • Pact with Darkness
  • Shadowforged Plating
  • Cloak of Shadows
  • Distortion

Rotations

Single Target

Phantasm annihilates bosses.

AoE / Multi-Target

Hellstorm, Deadly Dancing, Apocalypse.

Pets & Belt

SlotRecommendation
Belt pets Cheap setup: 2 unicorn S3, 2 kenta s0, Snowman → worth sacrificing 1 unicorn for P.hector card
Normal setup: 3 Mino S3, 2 unicorns S5, Snowman
Expensive setup: 3 x Death Gladiators S0, 2 snowman/2 cubes/2 S3 picks (depends on what you need)
Summoned pet Bloodthirsty Slaughterer or Wind Pixie (depends on the content)

Gear

Progression Path

  1. R2 helmet +20 (all skills +1)
  2. R6 +15 gear from hidden sanctuary
  3. Circus weapons and accessories from Circus dungeon
  4. Parallel World gear (either trash stats 2 slots with Phase blades, or max slots in each gear but trash skill, depends on what you could find)
  5. Island of gods weapons and rings
  6. DD Accessories and Yushiva belt
  7. Lava gear (boots, gloves, armor, helmet)
  8. Medal
  9. Emblem

Optimization

You upgrade the above when it's applicable e.g., more slots in belt, +24 weapon, +24 medusa helmet, better stats on gear. You always prioritize stats like the following item:

Perfect Sword Example (image removed)

Example of purple stats you look for:

  • Evasion and movement speed on boots | Evasion or CP on armor, Emblem, Medal
  • Accuracy on gloves, medal or emblem

Leveling Progression

  • Starting with Rappelz
  • Master Class and Beyond
  • 175 and Beyond

Tips

  • You can always switch your Bloodthirsty Slaughterer with a Wind Pixie if you find issues tanking.
  • Lvl 2 potion of the above mentioned pets is a huge boost.
  • When you rotate through rooms in DD6 for example, use Deadly Dancing in a room then Apocalypse + Hellstorm in the other room. Don't use all AOEs in 1 room or you'll end up losing so much time in the next one (this is of course if you can afford killing mobs with 1 or 2 skills only).
  • If you ever pulled mobs by mistake don't run, you have Cloak of Shadows and Distortion!
  • Use Gloom Aura to pull 1 room, then Smoke Bomb to pull the next one.
