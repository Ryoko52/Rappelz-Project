Note

Wait, where is the main skill? No important skill is worth the investment into gear for now, which is why everyone is waiting for a rebalance of this class. But if you had to pick one, Recklessness of Earth is recommended , it makes the buff reduce more damage taken and last longer, improving survivability, which is exactly what this class lacks.

Gameplay

Weapon choice: 2H Axe.

Rotation 1: Cast all Recklessness skills + Impaling Power + Focused Gust , then cast Gravity Amplification on mobs, then Shuddering Flames .

Rotation 2: Cast Huge Rock Fall , then Huge Volcanic Eruption + Rain of Spirit .

Gear

Progression Path

  1. R2 helmet +20 (all skills +1)
  2. R6 +15 gear from hidden sanctuary
  3. Circus weapons and accessories from Circus dungeon
  4. Parallel World gear (either trash stats 2 slots with recklessness of earth, or max slots in each gear but trash skill, depends on what you could find)
  5. Island of gods weapons and rings
  6. DD Accessories and Yushiva belt
  7. Lava gear (boots, gloves, armor, helmet)
  8. Medal
  9. Emblem

Optimization

You upgrade the above when it's applicable e.g., more slots in belt, +24 weapon, +24 medusa helmet, better stats on gear.

Example of purple stats you look for:

  • Evasion and movement speed on boots | Evasion or CP on armor, Emblem, Medal
  • Accuracy on gloves, medal or emblem

Tips

01
Even if this class is not Agility based, Evasion helps tremendously, don't hesitate to try Wind Pixie + Wind Pixie pot if you want more survival.
02
You can sacrifice M.Atk on belt for more Evasion on belt; it works wonders.
03
+10 cards on Recklessness with a +25 helmet makes Recklessness available 24/7, which means you can no longer die with additional buffs like Gnoll Buff, Camper, Deva Blessing Lv2, Obidikar Card and Arcane Guardian , the class becomes A-tier (though it still suffers from Long CDs).
