Gameplay

Very simple class, no big brain moves.

Opener: Radiant Flare

Use Mass Repentance and Shining Cross, they have very low CD. Shining Buster is an insane burst, save it for bosses.

Use Divine Shield during boss encounters!

Gear

Progression Path

  1. R2 helmet +20 (all skills +1)
  2. R6 +15 gear from hidden sanctuary
  3. Circus weapons and accessories from Circus dungeon
  4. Parallel World gear (either trash stats 2 slots with your main skill, or max slots in each gear but trash skill, depends on what you could find)
  5. Island of gods weapons and rings
  6. DD Accessories and Yushiva belt
  7. Lava gear (boots, gloves, armor, helmet)
  8. Medal
  9. Emblem

Optimization

You upgrade the above when it's applicable e.g., more slots in belt, +24 weapon, +24 medusa helmet, better stats on gear.

Example of purple stats you look for:

  • Evasion and movement speed on boots | Evasion or CP on armor, Emblem, Medal
  • M.accuracy on gloves, medal or emblem
