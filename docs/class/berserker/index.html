Berserker

B Tier

Overview

RoleDPS / Subtank
DifficultyMedium
StrengthsHigh HP pool and invincibility spell (similar to Mercenary's immortality)
WeaknessesHas no armor penetration passives, which makes it weaker than most classes
Why Play ItBarbarian-feel class, thirsty to cut something with an Axe!

Racial

Gameplay

Weapon choice: 2H Axe for more skill damage.

Use Zephyr Strike or Earth Shaker to apply Tactical Advantage, then use your AoE skills.

Rotation 1: Earth Shaker + Thunderbolt Wind Cut + Shuddering Flame

Rotation 2: Zephyr Strike + Whirlwind Assault + Energy Bomb + Thunderbolt Wind Cut

Healing order: Always use Energy Charge first to consume all Spiracles, then use Spiracle Rush . If you use Spiracle Rush while you still have Spiracles, you will not heal anything.

Gear

Progression Path

  1. R2 helmet +20 (all skills +1)
  2. R6 +15 gear from hidden sanctuary
  3. Circus weapons and accessories from Circus dungeon
  4. Parallel World gear (either trash stats 2 slots with Phase blades, or max slots in each gear but trash skill, depends on what you could find)
  5. Island of gods weapons and rings
  6. DD Accessories and Yushiva belt
  7. Lava gear (boots, gloves, armor, helmet)
  8. Medal
  9. Emblem

Optimization

You upgrade the above when it's applicable e.g., more slots in belt, +24 weapon, +24 medusa helmet, better stats on gear.

Example of purple stats you look for:

  • Evasion and movement speed on boots | Evasion or CP on armor, Emblem, Medal
  • Accuracy on gloves, medal or emblem
