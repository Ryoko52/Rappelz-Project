Dungeon Guide

Devildom

Lv 175+ Kaia Lake 60 Rooms 6 Stages

Devildom is a dungeon for players who are at least level 175. Complete the Ice Maiden quest line first, then the friendly Marduka Nakibu will offer a short quest line that unlocks dungeon access. Located in the centre of Kaia Lake.

Dungeon Structure

Devildom is made up of 60 rooms split across 6 stages, each harder than the last. You must kill all monsters in a room before moving to the next.

Stages & Bosses

Stages 1–3 have fixed boss spawns. Stages 4–5 and Stage 6's first room each have a 50% chance to spawn the default pair or the Main Boss + Yushiva. Stage 6's final room is always a fixed multi-boss encounter.

Stage Warps & NPC Nuz

Complete a quest for each stage from Nuz (outside the dungeon) to unlock direct warping. You arrive in the stage's Lobby — talk to the stone inside to enter. Party members can run through stages they haven't unlocked yet when following someone who has.

Yushiva's Belt

Grants +30 Critical Power and +1 level to all Skills (excluding TP skills). Provides damage bonuses vs. specific monster types depending on the Belt Stone applied.

Default: +20% damage dealt to Spirit type · −20% damage received from Spirit type.

With Belt Stone (Cash Shop): +20% dealt to Spirits + one extra type · −10% received from those two types.

Yushiva's Accessories Set Bonus

Equipping both Yushiva's Necklace and Yushiva's Red Earrings grants a set bonus, further enhanced when also wearing a Yushiva's Belt.

Crystals & Soul Beads

All mobs and bosses can drop Black Crystal Boxes and Spirit Bead Boxes.

  • Black Crystal Box → 1 Crystal of Devildom. Collect 100 to craft 1 Refined Crystal (double-click to combine).
  • Spirit Bead Box → 1 random Soul Bead:
    • White Dragon's Soul Bead — most common
    • Black Dragon's Soul Bead — relatively common
    • Sand Dragon's Soul Bead — rare

Blacksmith Tarun

Find Blacksmith Tarun in Katan to craft all Devildom armor sets. Tarun can also open Black Crystal Boxes in bulk and sells the Medusa's Shaper — converts any helmet into a Medusa's Helm (required for Devildom helm crafts), letting you transfer enchantment levels.

Scales of Devildom & Devil Orbs

Scales of Devildom — needed for Inferno. Combine Refined Crystals with Black Scale pieces dropped by Stage 4–6 bosses.

Devil Orbs — needed for Reviac. Drop from Stage 6 bosses. A daily quest rewards a box containing either a Devil Orb or a Devil Orb Piece — combine 10 pieces to make one Orb.

Devildom Cloaks

Three cloaks crafted from Devildom materials. Craft the White or Black cloak first — one is required to make the Sand Dragon's Cloak. Enhanced using Dark Cubes — available from the Cash Shop or purchasable with Luciad Medals from the Rondo Citadel NPC. White and Black cap at +10; Sand goes to +20.

Job Type Note

Cross-Class Upgrading

Nusylite and Fluminis can be worn by all job types, allowing cross-class Parallel World upgrades (e.g. a War Kahuna wearing Berserker-exclusive Mach's Armor upgraded to Nusylite).

Once upgraded to Inferno, equipment becomes job-type specific again — plan which character does the upgrade before spending materials.
