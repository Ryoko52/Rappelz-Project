Epic Dungeon Guide

Alcatraz

Lv 228–230 Marduka Watchtower 8 Difficulties 1–8 Players

A high-level Epic dungeon. The Butcher Vulcan has led a prison break threatening Gaia — rally the guilds and retake Alcatraz. Unlock it via the questline from Guard Captain Kudun in Rondo (Lv 228); enter through Sorcerer Kael at the Marduka Watchtower (Lv 230).

Dungeon Entrance

Enter through the Marduka Watchtower via Sorcerer Kael. Talk to him to create the instance and pick your Player Mode.

Marduka Watchtower — Dungeon Entrance

Difficulty & Player Modes

When creating a dungeon, you must first select the Player Mode. Each Player Mode grants access to a specific set of dungeon stages. Completing an available stage rewards the corresponding Bonus Chest listed below.

  • 1 Player Mode: Stages 1, 2, 4, and 5 (Bonuses 1, 2, 4, and 5).
  • 2 Player Mode: Stages 2, 3, 4, and 6 (Bonuses 1, 2, 3, and 5).
  • 4 Player Mode: Stages 3, 5, 6, and 7 (Bonuses 2, 3, 4, and 5).
  • 8 Player Mode: Stages 4, 6, 7, and 8 (Bonuses 2, 3, 5, and 6).

Dungeon Monsters

Every stage uses the same enemies — only their level rises with the stage. You'll fight waves of prison mobs plus three mini-bosses and the final boss.

Regular Mobs

Brutal, Cunning, Crazy, Chain, Cursed, Furious & Vengeful Prisoners, Prisoner of Darkness, Herald of Darkness, Follower of Pain, Cursed Soul, Summoned Prisoner.

Bosses

Karmos Saragorn Necron mini-bosses, then the final boss Butcher Vulcan.

Loot & Drops

Loot depends on whether you defeat normal monsters or bosses. Normal monsters provide materials and progression items, while each boss also drops unique equipment, crafting materials, and its own Boss Card.

Accessory Enchanting

Enchanting Alcatraz accessories follows the same principle as Sky Accessories.

SlotRequirement
Main SlotThe accessory you want to enchant
Material SlotStat Essence × (target enchant × 10)
Material SlotImbuement Ore or Blessed Imbuement Ore × 1

Essence cost example: going from +9 to +10 requires 100 Stat Essence (10 × 10).

Base accessories can be enchanted up to +10. Enhanced versions can go up to +15.

Alcatraz Imbuement Eraser

The Alcatraz Imbuement Eraser removes only the applied Imbuement Essence, so you can swap to a different stat while keeping the accessory, its enhancements, and its Awakening intact.

Sapphire Transfer Scroll

The Sapphire Transfer Scroll lets you transfer a Sapphire Ring, Earring, or Necklace into a new accessory. Speak to Blacksmith Elara outside Alcatraz.

Blacksmith Elara — found outside Alcatraz

Requirements

  • The Sapphire Transfer Scroll in your inventory.
  • The Sapphire Ring / Earring / Necklace you want to transfer equipped.
  • Combination materials in your inventory: 50 Mithril Ore + 1 accessory-specific item:
    • Necron's Shadow Shard
    • Karmos's Blood-Tainted Fang
    • Saragorn's Alembic Vial

How to Transfer

Talk to Blacksmith Elara outside Alcatraz and choose "Sapphire Transfer" from the menu.
You'll only be shown equipped accessories eligible for transfer. To avoid mistakes, only equip the one you want transferred.
Click the accessory you wish to transfer and confirm.

Boss Card Collection

Each boss has a chance to drop its own Boss Card. Collect the required number and trade them to the Black Marketer Nostra Family NPC for a stronger Collection Card.
