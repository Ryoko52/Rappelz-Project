Epic Dungeon Guide

Alcatraz

Lv 228–230 Marduka Watchtower 8 Difficulties 1–8 Players

Alcatraz is a high-level Epic dungeon. A prison break led by The Butcher Vulcan threatens the Kingdom of Gaia, and adventurers must rally the guilds and retake the prison. Access is unlocked through a questline starting from Guard Captain Kudun in Rondo (Lv 228), and the dungeon itself is entered via Sorcerer Kael at the Marduka Watchtower (Lv 230).

Dungeon Entrance

The dungeon is entered through the Marduka Watchtower, where Sorcerer Kael guards the secret passage into the prison. Speak to the entrance NPC to create an instance and select your Player Mode before entering.

Difficulty & Player Modes

Alcatraz offers 8 difficulty levels, selected by speaking to the entrance NPC before entering. The chosen Player Mode also sets the maximum number of players allowed inside the instance.

Player limit is enforced. If a mode is capped at 2 players and a third tries to enter, the extra player is automatically removed from the instance.

Fewer-player bonus. Clearing a difficulty with fewer players than allowed grants better bonus rewards. On defeating the final boss, players have a chance at additional bonus loot from a Bonus Chest.

Each Player Mode grants access to a specific set of dungeon stages. Completing an available stage rewards the matching Bonus Chest. Stages not listed for a mode are locked (marked X) and cannot be selected.

Alcatraz Imbuement Eraser

To change the imbuement stat on your accessory without losing the accessory itself — or its valuable Awakening — use an Alcatraz Imbuement Eraser.

The Eraser removes only the applied Imbuement Essence, letting you apply a different essence while keeping the same accessory and all of its existing enhancements and Awakening intact.

Enhanced Mithril Accessories

Using an Alcatraz Imbuement Eraser on an Enhanced Mithril Accessory removes the Imbuement Essence, but the accessory reverts to an Enhanced Mithril Accessory +0.

Sapphire Transfer Scroll

The Sapphire Transfer Scroll lets you transfer a Sapphire Ring, Earring, or Necklace into a new accessory. Speak to Blacksmith Elara outside Alcatraz.

Requirements

  • The Sapphire Transfer Scroll in your inventory.
  • The Sapphire Ring / Earring / Necklace you want to transfer equipped.
  • Combination materials in your inventory: 50 Mithril Ore + 1 accessory-specific item:
    • Necron's Shadow Shard
    • Karmos's Blood-Tainted Fang
    • Saragorn's Alembic Vial

How to Transfer

Talk to Blacksmith Elara outside Alcatraz and choose "Sapphire Transfer" from the menu.
You'll only be shown equipped accessories eligible for transfer. To avoid mistakes, only equip the one you want transferred.
Click the accessory you wish to transfer and confirm.
