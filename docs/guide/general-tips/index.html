General Tips & Tricks

Essential advice for efficient leveling, gear progression, and gameplay optimization.

Strategic Leveling Tips

Tip Key Point
Survivability over Damage Most players rush for damage but then complain about dying. Prioritize survivability while gearing up at low levels. A Soul of Lunacy card is one of the cheapest and best choices for most classes without healing skills.
Vulc Questline Timing Start the daily Vulc questline early. Koala spawns can be unpredictable, and you need the Koala quest to unlock 190+ Vulc content and get free +1 upgrade cubes.
Keep R2+20 Helmet Hold onto your R2+20 helmet and keep gear in Distorted (160) until you can afford a +20 Medusa/Nusy helmet. The +1 all skills bonus is worth more than the set bonus.
Farm at RoA Until 180 Stay at Remains of Ancients until level 180. Well-geared players can kill in Hard Mode RoA by level 175. Killing snowmen or in Hector room drops 1-3 cubes for passive income, which also covers crafting materials later.
Free vs Paid Powerlevels Avoid spending Rupees on powerlevels, but always take free ones. They're most impactful in the <170 and 180-190 level ranges.
Smart Vendor Choices Sell ND (non-disassembleable) weapons and armor to vendors, but disassemble or altar dura gear. Crit power deco pieces from the altar are far more valuable than vendor rupees, especially before getting Lava gear.

Gameplay Optimization

Mechanic How It Works
Gnoll Buff 30%/40%/50% damage reflection reduces damage taken by 3%/4%/5%. Very helpful buff for survivability in harder dungeons.
Camper Title Reduces damage taken. Buy a tent from the merchant to earn this title.
Weapon Awakening Always awaken weapons and accessories with Awakening Stones. Keep weapons with the best bonus for future use.
Universal Upgrades Priority Prioritize universal upgrades over class-specific gear (Lava Set, Helmet +24, Emblem, Yushiva Belt). You never know when you'll want to change classes.
Purse Purchases Buy Purse of Bright Powers (non-pet classes) or Purse of Invocations (pet classes) for 500 Cash Shop coins - the bonus is worth the investment.
Enhanced Crystal Glasses Cheap item equippable from level 1: +10 all stats, +40 luck. Get from AH for ~25M (drops from HRoA snowman Hector room). Get it ASAP.
Arena Currency Once committed to a main, do daily Arena quests. 20k Arena points = Traveling Gear (4% all stats) from Arena Merchant Jana at Rondo. Worth the grind.
