175 and Beyond

Level 175 - 180: Devildom (DD)

Devildom is the primary farming dungeon for this level range. Enter via the Kaia chain quest.

Difficulty Experience Notes
DD2 1 hour SS = 20% exp You should be able to solo this easily with previous setup.
DD3 Much faster exp Possible for most classes - easier for some than others. Class-dependent difficulty.
DD4 Highest difficulty Completely different scale. Don't get demotivated if you can't clear yet.

Key Drops: Crystals for gear upgrades and DD accessories (8 types that you must collect same as you did for Circus Accessories: Spirit, Undead, Mecca, Beasts, Ains, Dragons, Devils, Antique)

Priority: Daily DD runs | Rupee farming | Acquire PW gear for your main class | Upgrade skill cards to +6/+7

Gear Target: At this phase you should be focusing on your dailies DD1/2/3, farming Rupees, finding better PW gear with your main class skill, and getting your skill cards +6/+7.
Once you do all of this, farm Rupees to buy Lava gear set (sometimes sold for 25B per piece - Head, Armor, Gloves, Boots) which is a universal upgrade usable for your future classes and can boost your damage by an insane amount.
- Lava weapon can be sold for 35B to 50B.
Buy buffs for 5B from Players => I can't stress enough how important this is. Once you test it you will be addicted to these buffs forever.

Target Gear for Level 175-180

Equipment Specifications
Belt Yushiva 8-slot (Antique stone)
Weapon +20 (1/32), upgrade to +25 (32/32) over time
Defense P.def not important past ~75k. Focus on Evasion: 8k+ (DE/Slayer), 4k+ (others)
Helmet +22 minimum with +2 all skills
Boss Cards Devil Skeleton (+30% damage type) | Reviac (+100 CP, -100% Crit) | P.Hector / M.Hector

Level 180 - 190

If you're able to solo DD4/DD5 after following the previous steps and meeting the target gear requirements, then you can level yourself all the way to 195 in less than 2 weeks. Your goal would be the following: - Crafting Sand Cloak (equipable lvl 190) - Crafting Reviac Gear => crafting guide is in official Discord If you leveled yourself then you will already have 50% of the needed materials to craft the gear. Always upgrade armor first:.

Upgrade Path Priority Notes
Boots, Gloves, Helmet Keep Current Maintain Nusy gear for these slots
Armor TOP PRIORITY Upgrade path: Nusy > Flumi > Inferno > Reviac
Reviac Helmet 2nd Priority Provides +10% evasion bonus
Remaining Gear 3rd Priority Complete Reviac set after armor and helmet

Target Gear for Level 180-190

Equipment Specifications
Cloak Sand Cloak +20 (upgrade with Dark Cubes)
Armor Full Reviac Gear with main class skill +1
Belt Yushiva Antique 8-slot (+22 minimum)
Weapon +24 or higher
Helmet +24 minimum
Decorative gear Full Lava Set ( without cloak & Mask since they are expensive and you can aim
to craft them yourself once you hit 200+ by doing Vulcano dailies
Rings +20 CP from Island of Gods, Awakened with CP
Accessories Full DD Accessories (all 8 types), Awakened with CP
Cards Devil Skeleton, Reviac Card, Hector/Lunacy

Level 190 - 220

If you don't have the gear listed above, you won't be able to do much in SF. Farming all of this will allow you to solo DD6 and level yourself all the way to 200+.

Target Gear for Level 190-210

Equipment Specifications
Accessories Sky Set (accessories + Chaos Stone), enhanced with your main stat. Stop at +6 or +7.
Helmet Luciad Iron Mask with +20 CP
Craftables Cloak, Advanced Wings, Mask - all gained from Vulcanos 210 x10 daily runs

Progression Note:
veteran players choose to join a powerlevel from 190 to 220 because it is the fastest and most efficient way to gain a boost in stats, more access to TPs, and unlocking Maze/SF and TU dailies ASAP. The other alternative is to join a guild and do SF runs together until you hit 220. .
