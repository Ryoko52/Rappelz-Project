Pre-requisite

You have to finish the "Vulcanus" Quest-Line and the first of the "Ancient Koala" Quest-Line to be able to receive the keys and enter the dungeon (unlocks at lvl 175).

The materials to craft the new Lava-Gear can be obtained from the Vulcanus dungeon east of Rondo, which can be entered after reaching level 200.

The basic wings can be enhanced with Lava cubes and the Adv. Wings can be enhanced with Adv. Lava Cubes.
If the enhancement of the Wings fail, the wings will NOT break but the invested Lava Cube will be lost.

Bonus Sets

If you have one of the new weapons, the armor, the helmet, the boots, the gloves and one of the 4 new wings equipped you gain the first set bonus:

  • +10% critical power
  • +15% max hp
  • reduces dmg received from pets by 10%

The mask and the cloak will give you the second set bonus:

Set For Mask and Cloak

  • DMG All Types: 10%
  • DMG Reduce All Types: 5%

To enhance the cloak you need the Lava Feather's.
If this enhancement fails, the cloak will break and you need to repair it with an "Lava repair kit" which can be obtained from the cash shop.
