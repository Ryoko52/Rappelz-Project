PATCH 9.8

How to Make a Pure White Weapon / Shield

With patch 9.8 you can now obtain an upgrade for your weapons from the Island of Forgotten Gods and shields from Devildom.

Crafting a Pure White Weapon / Shield

Combine the following three items at the upgrade NPC:

  • x1 Island of Forgotten Gods Weapon or Devildom Shield (your existing item)
  • x1 Clean Circus Dungeon Weapon or Shield (same type)
  • Pure Luciad’s Energy — 25 for a 1-handed weapon / shield  •  50 for a 2-handed weapon

Luciad’s Power — New Maximum

Once upgraded, the maximum enchantment level via Luciad’s Power is increased from +32 to +40.

RangeMaterial Required
+0 → +32Luciad’s Power (standard)
+32 → +40Luciad’s Advanced Cube — low drop chance from the new dungeon

Upgrade Retention

When upgrading your existing weapon or shield, everything is preserved:

  • Enchantment level
  • Awakening
  • Random options
  • Soulstones
  • Weapon shards

Requirement

⚠  Level 210 required. When upgrading to a Pure White Weapon or Shield, the level requirement to equip the item is raised to Level 210.
