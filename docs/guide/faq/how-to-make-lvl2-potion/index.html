How to Make a Level 2 Potion

Requirements

  • 1 × Empty Level 2 Potion — dropped during certain events
  • 1 × Level 190 Pet
The Empty Level 2 Potion is not available from regular merchants — it is only obtainable as a drop from specific in-game events.

How It Works

When your pet reaches level 190, it unlocks a Level 2 Creation skill. Make sure you have an Empty Level 2 Potion in your bag, then use that skill to fill it.
