Enhanced Teardrops (+5 / +10)

Enhanced Teardrops can be leveled up to +10 via the Soul Crafter NPC.

+1 to +5 — Soul Stone Essence

Soul Stone Essence — dropped in the Maze Dungeon.

Use Soul Stone Essence to upgrade your Enhanced Teardrops from +1 up to +5.

+5 to +10 — Volatile Soul Stone Essence

Volatile Soul Stone Essence — available from the Cash Shop.

Use Volatile Soul Stone Essence to upgrade from +5 to +10.

Volatile Soul Stone Essence can technically be used from +1 to +10, but it is a Cash Shop item. Save it for the +5 → +10 range where the free alternative is not available.

Recommendation

  • Use Soul Stone Essence (Maze Dungeon drop) from +1 to +5
  • Use Volatile Soul Stone Essence (Cash Shop) from +5 to +10
