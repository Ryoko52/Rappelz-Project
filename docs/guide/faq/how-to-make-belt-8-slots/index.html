How to Make Belt (8 Slots)

Guide to upgrading your belt from slot 5 up to slot 8 using the Ultimate Belt Upgrade Kit.

⚠  Important: The Ultimate Belt Upgrade Kit is only used for slots 5, 6, 7, and 8.
Lower slots (1–4) require the regular Belt Upgrade Kit, which can be purchased from a merchant in Hidden Village or dropped from mobs.

Overview

The Ultimate Belt Upgrade Kit is used to upgrade belts from slot 5 → slot 8, one slot at a time.

Requirements

  • The belt you want to upgrade (placed in the target slot)
  • 1 × Ultimate Belt Upgrade Kit (placed in the material slot)

How It Works

  1. Place the belt in the target slot.
  2. Place 1 Ultimate Belt Upgrade Kit in the material slot.
  3. Attempt the upgrade.

Result

✓  Success: The belt gains one additional slot.
