Beyond Master Class

Level progression from 153 to 175 with optimized gear and farming strategies.

Level 153–160: Early Endgame

Choose one of the following methods based on your class strength and gear:

Method Location Difficulty Description
Method 1 UG Quests Easy Take from village teleport NPCs. Pop Stamina Saver and Growth potions. Spam UG 2 Palmir until level 160.
Method 2 Snow Basin Medium Kill dragons until level 160. Best for Void Mage or Berserker (AoE abilities make it easier to kite).
Method 3 Labyrinth Dungeon Medium Do quests to final boss summoning Butkada and Akshas. Take quest → kill boss → abandon. +5% exp per run.
Method 4 Island of Gods Hard & Fast Buy Boarding Pass from Black Marketer. 30-min solo to level 160. Not recommended for new players - alts only.

Best Leveling Location: Remains of Ancients (RoA)

To enter: Complete a quest at Circus 3 (starts at City of Ruins Hector NPC).

If you followed the previous steps, RoA will be easy for you. Farm here for an hour or more - this step defines your future leveling speed for years to come.

Circus Drops (Game Changers)

Circus 3 drops Necklace + Earrings with bonus damage and damage reduction to mobs. Highest priority types: Undead, Beast, and Mecca. You'll need 2 earrings + 1 necklace of the same type to switch depending on the mobs you face in RoA.

Pro tip: If your class can handle Circus 4, go for it - the drops are much better.
