CLASS TIER LIST

9.10
TIER CLASS NOTES
S Mercenary Beginner-friendly and easy to learn.
S Deadeye Strongest class right now due to no skill CD, high evasion, and high damage.
S Slayer Strongest melee class, high damage, and an easier way to sustain health than Deadeye.
S Void Mage Hard in the early game, but in the end game it becomes equivalent to Deadeye and Slayer.
A Master Breeder Weaker than Overlord in the early game, then becomes stronger at 200+, and is hard to play in 220+ content.
A Overlord Strongest pet class in the early game and highly recommended for beginners who want to farm Devildom with a small budget as a first character.
B Magus Very strong mage class, high damage, but lack of HP and threat reduction makes it a solo class and not favorable for party play.
B Cardinal Very strong mage class and performs very well across game content until end game, with better defensive capabilities than Magus.
B Templar The best and only tank in the game, very needed for party and guild play, especially in the end game.
C Berserker High health, medium damage, cool AoE skills, and lacks defensive capabilities except for a 5-minute CD skill.
C Beast Master Best rupee farmer in the game, but weaker for game content than other pet classes. Very weak class and needs an urgent rebalance.
C War Kahuna Very high damage, but lack of HP makes it unplayable in late game unless you meet all requirements to reach 100% damage reduction, which costs a lot.
D Corruptor Very weak class and needs an urgent rebalance.
D Marksman Very weak class and needs an urgent rebalance.
D Oracle Support class with very impactful toggles, recommended only if you start the game as a duo or trio, and unable to farm game content efficiently as a beginner.
